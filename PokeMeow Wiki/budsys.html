Buddy Feature

+

- You can set any Pokemon as your buddy and gain and by catching Pokemon, completing quests , hatching eggs, and eventually battling.
- Once you set a Pokemon as your buddy, it will be assigned random IVs for ATK, DEF, HP, and SPEED. These IVs range from 0-15 for Regular/Shiny Pokemon and 0-20 for Golden Pokemon.
- You can reroll specific IVs at a cost of 1 vote coin/reroll. Uncommon+ Patreon supporters have the ability to view specific IV numbers for their buddies.
- You can gain exp for Pokemon in your team using an EXP Share as well, which is buyable from the shop for a hefty price. The exp share gives bonus exp to all members of your team (excluding your buddy if it is also on your team).
- All buddies start off with 2 moves. You can buy new moves for your buddy from ;moves, which will be useful for battling! More buyable moves will be added to the Pokemon as it is still a work in progress.
- IVs, Friendship, Level, and XP can be inherited by the evolution (if you have a profile for it) if the values are higher from the pre-evolution.

Evolution Feature

+

- Gaining will level up your Pokemon, which increases their stats and allows you to evolve the Pokemon once you reach the required level (if it has an evolution).
- Some Pokemon require friendship or evolution stones to evolve, while others simply have a level requirement.
- Evolving a Pokemon that has more than 1 different evolution will randomize what you get from evolving (i.e. if you evolve an Eevee, you have an equal chance to obtain any particular evolution of it, as long as it's in the game).
- You can receive Rare Candies from lootboxes, at half the rate of Masterballs. Rare candies increase the level of your buddy by 1.
Note that Rare Candies doesn't upgrade the Level of a pokemon

How to Select a Buddy

+

Type ;buddy set #(number of the pokemon you want as buddy).

Minimum and Maximum catches required to hatch an Egg

+

Eggs reach the READY TO HATCH! state anywhere between 30 to 130 catches.
