When are new Golden Promo Pokemon released

+

Golden Pokemon are released on special occasions, such as Server Milestones, special Holidays/celebrations, or certain birthdays.

Server Milestones: New Golden Pokemon are released when PokeMeow reaches certain server count milestones. These milestones include:
- 500, 1,000, 2,500, 5,000, 10,000, 25,000, 50,000, 100,000.
- Future promos will be decided at different numbers (possibly every 50k after 100,000)

Holidays/Celebrations: These include: Easter, Halloween, Christmas & New Years, as well as yearly anniversaries for PokeMeow.

Who decides about Promo?

+

Generally, Golden Promo's are decided by Gopi. For Birthdays, they are decided by the birthday of the staff member. Shiny Patreon supporters also have the ability to decide Golden Promo's (for milestone/holiday promos only).

How do I Get a Golden Larvitar or the Active Trainer role?

+

Active contributors with meaningful contributions to suggestions/bug reports/helping others or activity in #general will be put into consideration for codes and the active trainer role by server staff. As a MINIMUM requirement, you need to have spent at least 7 days (probably closer to a month since the server is growing fast) in the support server to have a chance at being considered. Note: Time spent in this server does not necessarily mean that you will get the role.

There are limits to how many codes will be given out for specific Pokemon. They are given out arbitrarily to users (could be multiple gifts in a day, 1 gift a week, or even just 1 gift a month depending on activity). The requirements for active trainer will likely see an increase as the server grows.

Do not beg for codes or argue why you deserve codes. If you are a consistent contributor to the support server and a frequent user of the bot in this server, you will eventually receive one for your dedication.
